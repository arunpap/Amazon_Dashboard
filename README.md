📊 Amazon Global Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Amazon's global sales performance across different markets, customer segments, products, and years. The dashboard provides actionable business insights into sales, profit, customer performance, product profitability, and regional distribution, helping stakeholders make informed business decisions.

The Amazon Global Sales Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution built using Power BI to visualize and analyze Amazon's sales data from 2012 to 2015. It enables users to monitor key business KPIs, compare yearly performance, identify profitable customers and products, analyze market-wise sales, and discover geographical sales trends through an intuitive and interactive dashboard.

🛠 Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
📊 Power BI Desktop – Used to design interactive reports and dashboards.
📂 Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created calculated measures and KPI metrics.
🔗 Data Modeling – Established relationships between multiple tables for accurate reporting.
📈 Interactive Visualizations – Cards, Pie Charts, Donut Charts, Bar Charts, Map Visuals, Slicers, and Filters.

The data represents Amazon's international business operations across multiple markets and customer segments, making it suitable for sales performance and business intelligence analysis.


📌 Business Problem
Large e-commerce businesses generate thousands of sales transactions daily across different countries, customer segments, and product categories.

Without an interactive reporting system, it becomes difficult to answer important business questions such as:
• Which market generates the highest sales?
• Who are the most profitable customers?
• Which products generate maximum profit?
• Which products are causing losses?
• How are sales distributed globally?
• How do yearly sales compare?
Traditional spreadsheets make answering these questions time-consuming and inefficient.


🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:
• Monitor overall business performance.
• Track yearly sales trends.
• Identify high-value customers.
• Analyze profitable and loss-making products.
• Compare sales across different markets.
• Visualize global business presence using geographical maps.
• Support business decision-making through interactive reporting.
